About

01

Hella Ella is the brainchild of Ella, a 20-year-old fashion lover based in Melbourne. What started as a love for thrifting turned into a mission to give pre-loved clothes the second life they deserve.

From vintage band tees to Y2K staples, every piece is hand-picked with raw, lived-in energy. You'll find her at fashion markets around Bendigo — curating racks of finds you won't see anyone else wearing. No fast fashion. No fakes. Just real style with real history.
